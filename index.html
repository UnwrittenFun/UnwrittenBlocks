Unwritten Blocks

Unwritten Blocks adds a collection of useful blocks and items to enhance your experience in modded Minecraft.

For example, wall teleporters allow you to teleport anywhere in your world including cross-dimension. They are full blocks that can be masked to look like any other block in the game to match with your base design, if you choose not to mask them then they have a green connected texture.
